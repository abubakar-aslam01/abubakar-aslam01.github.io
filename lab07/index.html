My Travelling Experience

I’ve been fortunate to explore some of the most beautiful destinations in northern Pakistan. My journey started from Naran where the mesmerizing Kaghan Valley and Saif-ul-Malook lake left me in awe. Moving towards Swat, I experienced lush green valleys and friendly local culture. The snow-covered slopes of Malam Jabba offered thrilling adventures and skiing fun. I also visited Harnoi Waterfall in Abbottabad — a peaceful spot surrounded by refreshing natural beauty. My trip concluded at Babusar Top, standing above the clouds, witnessing one of the most breathtaking views of Pakistan. Each destination taught me something new — from the serenity of mountains to the joy of exploring new cultures. Travelling has made me more grateful, inspired, and connected to the world.

About My University

I am currently studying at Quaid-i-Azam University (QAU), one of the most prestigious institutions in Pakistan. QAU is located in Islamabad, surrounded by the Margalla Hills, offering a calm and academic environment. The Department of Computer Science provides excellent learning and research opportunities.
